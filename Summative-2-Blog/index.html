Personas & User Stories

Michael Deen

  • Age: 18
  • Occupation: Student
  • Status: Single
  • Location: New Zealand

    • GOALS, research and save up for a drone, participate in a drone race.

    USER JOURNEY, It's been a long week Michael decides to take a well earned break and (as per usual) spends his saturday playing video games. A couple hours drift by and Michael starts to get anxious as exams begin to creep back into his consciousness. Even with taking a break through gaming he can’t shake it and decides to do 30 minute increments of study, then games and repeat. Being a visual learner Micheal prefers watching youtube videos as tutorials for maths and english. Unfortunately he gets distracted and starts watching drones racing videos. Halfway through he gets an ad for a drone convention. Intrigued he clicks, and is guided to there website. Navigating through the photography and video is impressive but not really what he is interested in. However he spot ‘Drone Racing’ on the nav bar and instinctively clicks. The website promises the user an exciting day out where you can race against other people and even purchase the same drone models you fly. The only issue Micheal notices is that the event is not very close and has no way of using public transport, he will have to borrow his mums car for this.

    ABOUT, Michael is in the last weeks of college and is preparing the long voyage into exams. He is a very anxious person and finds he can get overwhelmed easily. Because of this, he spends a lot of time procrastinating from work that needs doing, mostly playing video games and watching tv shows. Michael’s favourite video games are racing and simulators. He really wants to try out racing in the real world but he can’t afford a car to start, let alone even attempt a driver's license. Recently he came across drone racing and has really gotten into it, and has decided to start saving for a drone.

    Jonathan Gump

  • Age: 27
  • Occupation: Camera Man

    USER JOURNEY, During a lunch break while filming an ad Jonathan is in a conversation with another camera man. Specifically the drone operator who they hired for the car ad they are currently working on. They are in a detailed discussion about their passion for filmmaking, during which Jonathan asks about the best way to become qualified for drone filmmaking as he did. From this he recommends trying out a cheaper consumer grade product and building your way up, followed by taking a qualification course. Jonathan is recommend to check out this drone convention that the other camera man goes too, and that it's great for all levels of expertise. Once filming is over Jonathan searches it on his phone. Once directed to the website he is greeted by a simple landing page. Most of the information he needs is there, but he wants more details about the particular shows or speakers but is hard pressed to find anything other than a simple blurb. Despite this he is curious and decides to clear his schedule and go.

    ABOUT, Jonathan is a professional cameraman who works for a wellington based studio. Being the quiet type Jonathan keeps to himself and doesn’t partake in many social gatherings, having a small friend group he prefers to hang out and stay indoors. He is also an avid technology geek. Always keeping up with the latest technologies through the internet. After a long day of work, Jon longs for nothing more than grabbing a beer, collapsing on the couch and watching useless youtube videos to help numb his brain. Being a cameraman he has always been interested in the rising drone technology. Currently, he is looking at investing in and learning how to fly a professional drone.
