about us

Drone Zone is a New Zealand owned convention that started three years ago. Our goal is to spread the fun and practicality of drones for personals and commercial use. We offer a range of activities, including tech exhibits, early access shopping, and racing. We strive to keep to our follow values:

back

professional

Our staff has a passion for what they do, and are known for their great professional attitude.

experience

Not only do we have plenty of experience, but we strive to give you a great experience to remember!

safety

When it comes to drone racing we take extra steps to ensure our customers safety. Race with that guarantee!

fun


Our founder Finn Patersonn made Drone zone to be a place of excitement and fun, for the whole family!
